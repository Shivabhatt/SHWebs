How It Works

From guest request to completed task, in seconds, not minutes.

Guests don't need to download anything or learn a new interface. They already have WhatsApp. SuperHospitality's AI concierge handles every request, understanding intent, identifying the right team member, and closing the loop automatically.

01

Guest sends a request

A guest texts, sends a voice note, or calls via WhatsApp. "I need extra towels," "Can I get late checkout?" Whatever they need, the guest can ask naturally.

02

AI understands intent

The AI concierge interprets the request, whether it's voice or text, and structures it into a clear, actionable task.

03

Right staff gets notified

Whether it's housekeeping, front desk, room service, or maintenance, the task goes directly to the team that can act on it. No manual relay.

04

Guest gets resolution

The task is completed and the guest is updated. Managers see everything in real time: response times, bottlenecks, and service quality.

The Problem Today

Hotel communication is broken by middlemen and manual processes.

Guests call the front desk. The front desk relays to housekeeping. Housekeeping misses the message. The guest calls again. Every layer adds delay, frustration, and information loss. SuperHospitality removes these bottlenecks entirely.

  • No more front desk acting as a switchboard for every single request.
  • No more lost messages between shifts or departments.
  • Full visibility for managers into what's requested, what's delayed, and what's resolved.
